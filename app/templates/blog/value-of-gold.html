Value of 24 Karat Gold Vs 22 Karat Gold

24 karat gold is pure gold, with a fineness of 999.9, meaning that it contains 99.99% pure gold. 22 karat gold has a fineness of 916, meaning that it contains 91.6% pure gold, and the rest is made up of other metals such as copper or silver.

In general, 24 karat gold will be more valuable than 22 karat gold due to its higher gold content. However, the value of gold is also determined by market conditions and the price of gold at a given time.

Another thing to consider is that 24 karat gold is softer and more delicate than 22 karat gold because of the addition of other metals in 22 karat gold which makes it harder. This can affect the durability and longevity of the jewellery or coins made out of it.
